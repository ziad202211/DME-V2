
# DM Enterprise Consulting — Bold & Corporate Redesign

## Overview
Redesign the DME Consulting website with a bold, corporate aesthetic while preserving all existing content. The site will use a hybrid structure: a strong homepage with all key sections, plus dedicated sub-pages for detailed project information.

---

## Homepage (Single Scroll)

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with a professional construction/engineering background image
- Bold "DM Enterprise" heading with a strong tagline
- Navigation bar at the top: HOME, ABOUT, SERVICES, PROJECTS, CAREERS
- Call-to-action scroll indicator

### 2. Services Section
Six service cards displayed in a responsive grid, each with an icon/image, title, and expandable bullet list:
- **Program Management** — Project Planning, SOP, Design Review, QA/QC, etc.
- **IT Solutions** — Network, Virtualization, SharePoint, Web Technologies, etc.
- **Project Controls** — Schedule Reviews, Claims Review, Risk Analysis, Earned Value, etc.
- **Construction Management** — Traffic Maintenance, Sediment & Erosion Control, Field Inspection, etc.
- **Geospatial Technologies** — GIS, Geodatabase Design, GPS Data Collection, etc.
- **Utility Engineering** — Infrastructure Rehab, Pumping Stations, AMI/AMR Water Meters, etc.

### 3. Projects Section
Showcase of featured projects in a visually appealing card layout:
- US Route 29 Transmission Water Main
- Cedar Lane Pumping Station
- Baltimore City Water Main Replacement/Rehab Program
- Wilde Lake

Each card will have an image and project name, linking to a dedicated project detail page.

### 4. Certifications Section
A clean row/grid displaying all certification logos and badges (MBE, DBE, SBE, etc.)

### 5. Footer
- DME logo
- Quick navigation links
- Contact information
- Copyright notice

---

## Sub-Pages

### About Page
- Company overview and mission

### Careers Page
- Career opportunities information

### Project Detail Pages
- Individual pages for each featured project with details and images

---

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Deep navy blue primary, gold/yellow accents (matching current branding), white backgrounds
- **Typography**: Strong, bold headings with clean body text
- **Layout**: Generous spacing, clear visual hierarchy, professional card-based design for services
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly with a hamburger menu on smaller screens
- **No backend needed**: Purely static content site
